What Is a Virtual Executive Assistant?

A Virtual Executive Assistant (VEA) is a senior-level remote professional who supports executives with strategic, operational, and administrative responsibilities.

Unlike general virtual assistants, a VEA handles:

  • Executive calendar management
  • High-level email triage
  • Meeting preparation and follow-ups
  • Client and stakeholder coordination
  • Travel and logistics
  • Process documentation
  • Confidential data handling

Remote Executive Assistant Services are designed for founders, CEOs, and leadership teams who need reliable operational support without hiring locally.
